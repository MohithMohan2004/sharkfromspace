Shark Habitats

  • Coral Reefs – Home to reef sharks in shallow, tropical waters.
  • Open Ocean – Pelagic species like makos and blue sharks roam here.
  • Deep Sea – Rare species like the goblin shark dwell in darkness.
  • Coastal Waters – Popular zones for bull and blacktip sharks.
